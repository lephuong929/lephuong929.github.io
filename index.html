About Me

I received my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a Marketing concentration from North Carolina State University in 2009. I worked in the banking industry for a few years, then I decided to pursue my Masters of Business Administration from The University of North Carolina at Wilmington. After completing my MBA, I started working at Cisco Systems as a UX Analyst where I was exposed to the basics of HTML and CSS for the first time. I worked closely with Business Analysts, UX Designers, Front-End and Back-End Developers to create and maintain the Cisco E-Commerce platform. As part of my role, I worked to maintain a consistent experience and design across the platform. I currently work at a digital marketing agency but have decided to attend the UNC Bootcamp to learn new skills and to pursue a career change.

While attending the UNC Bootcamp, I have further enhanced my skills in HTML and CSS. Additionally, I have learned Javascript, react, node.
